A galaxy can range in the number of stars as well as its size.<br></p> <em><p>The most famous Galaxies are : Milky Way, Andromeda Galaxy and Whirlspool Galaxy.</p></em>

In approximately 4.5 billion years the Andromeda Galaxy and the Milky Way are expected to collide and the result will be a giant elliptical galaxy.</p>
